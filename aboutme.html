Alice


Hairstylist


Alice grew up in New York apprenticing under Vidal Sassoon’s protégés as well as Sally Hershberger herself. She has worked on countless editorial shoots—for major publications including Vanity Fair to Allure—as well as on a vast array of blockbuster films like Twilights and the Blade trilogy. In addition, she’s appeared on numerous makeover shows. Highly sought after for her exceptional level of skill, Alice continues to be sought after by high-profile clientele, including Meg Ryan, Nicole Kidman, Michelle Williams, Jessica Biel, Mandy Moore, Michele Monaghan, Mary-Kate Olsen, Keri Russell, Jamie King, Ryan Reynolds and Ewan McGregor.
